The App Clip didn't open

You scanned the SoundingTV sign-in code on your iPhone, but iOS landed here instead of opening the App Clip card.

Open this on your iPhone

SoundingTV signs you in with an iPhone App Clip. Open this URL on an iPhone (or scan the QR code on your Apple TV with the Camera app).

Things to try

  • Make sure you're on the same Wi-Fi network as your Apple TV.
  • Restart the SoundingTV app on the Apple TV to refresh the sign-in code, then scan again.
  • If a "Local Network" permission prompt appears in the App Clip, allow it.
  • Update iOS to 16 or later if you're on an older version.

What you'll need

  • iPhone running iOS 16 or later, on the same Wi-Fi as the Apple TV.
  • The QR code shown by the SoundingTV app on your Apple TV.
  • The iPhone Camera will offer to open the App Clip when it scans the code.
